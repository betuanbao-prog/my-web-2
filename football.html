About Football

Overview

Football, known as soccer in North America, is the world's most popular sport. Played by over 250 million athletes in more than 200 countries, football combines skill, strategy, teamwork, and athleticism. The sport is governed internationally by FIFA and is played professionally at the highest levels across Europe, South America, Asia, and beyond. With its simple rules and universal appeal, football has become a global phenomenon that unites billions of fans across continents.

Key Rules

Football is played between two teams of 11 players each on a rectangular pitch. The objective is to score goals by directing the ball into the opposing team's net. Players can use any part of their body except their hands and arms (except the goalkeeper). A standard match lasts 90 minutes, divided into two 45-minute halves. The team with the most goals at the end wins the match.
